About Us

WE ARE DYNAMIC TEAM AND BUSINESS AGENCY

Our newly established workshop covers an area of 1,000 square meters, which is equipped with numerical-controlled processing center, casting machine, buff department, turning machines and packing apparatus. Our products are manufactured strictly according to the our predefined standards. We insist the basic idea of "Customers First, Clients' Satisfaction the Aim" and adhere to the principle of "Keeping Promise, Quality First, Considerate Service and Clients Supreme.
