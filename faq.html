FAQ.

How do I select cabinet hardware for my kitchen?

Choosing hardware for your cabinets is a personal decision. What you should know is that you can’t go wrong when you choose Kuldevi to complete the look of your space. We have a strong network of local showrooms with professional designers that we recommend working with. To find a Kuldevi dealer near you, please use our Dealer Locator tool.

Can I use different Kuldevi and pulls in the same design space?

Absolutely! This is exactly the reason why Kuldevi products are organized into collections. Within a collection you can mix and match Kuldevi and pulls of various designs, sizes, and finishes and still get a unified style in your kitchen.

Do Kuldevi products ship with screws?

All Kuldevi products ship with two different screws. One is 1 inch long and fits most cabinets and some drawers. The other is 1 ¾ inches long for extra thick cabinets and most drawers. This screw has sections that allow you the ability to customize the length to fit the thickness of your cabinet. If the screws we provide do not work with your specific cabinets, please contact our Customer Service team for a list of additional lengths available for purchase.

I am replacing existing hardware on my cabinets – how do I find an item that fits?

Absolutely! This is exactly the reason Kuldevi has the most extensive selection of Kuldevi and pulls in the industry. We will have one that fits most applications. Kuldevi are easy – it’s one hole. Pulls can get a bit more complicated because you need to match the center-to-center distance between the screw holes. We have tools that you may find helpful. Refine your selection is located on the left of each page. When searching for pulls, click on Center to Center to find your size.

What materials are used to manufacture your hardware?

Absolutely! This is exactly the reason We proudly use a wide range of high-quality materials to manufacture our products. You can find the materials listed on the detail page on the product. What you will find is that we use high-grade materials like solid zinc alloy, brass, crystal, and stainless steel as examples.

What is the warranty for Kuldevi products?

We manufacture beautiful products that you will enjoy for many years. In fact, we are so confident in our product that we offer a Lifetime Warranty for the original purchaser on all our Kuldevi products - against defects in materials and craftsmanship. Full details are listed on our Lifetime Warranty page.
